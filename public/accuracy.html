How We Measure Accuracy

Our accuracy metrics are based entirely on user feedback, collected during each morning check-in.

  • Resonance Rate: "Yes" + "Somewhat" responses as % of total feedback
  • Risk Accuracy: Correlation between predicted risk level and reported mood
  • ARL Learning: Each feedback response adjusts your personal model
  • No Cherry-Picking: All predictions are tracked, not just successful ones

Note: Accuracy improves over time as the system learns your unique patterns through ARL (Adaptive Resonance Learning).
