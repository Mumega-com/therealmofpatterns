# The Realm of Patterns — Vision

## Core Identity

**What this is:** A daily depth psychology practice. An 8-dimensional natal engine meets Jungian interpretation, met again every day by what the user actually reports about their inner state.

**What this is NOT:** An astrology app. A horoscope generator. A wellness tracker. A self-help tool.

---

## The Founding Insight

The natal chart is not fate. It is the particular psychological task you came here to engage.

Most astrology apps describe your personality. We don't. We use your birth data to compute an 8-dimensional psychological profile — one that maps to the Jungian tradition's understanding of the psyche as a field of autonomous forces. Then, every day, those forces meet the current sky and what you just told us about your inner state. The result is a reading that is genuinely about you, today.

---

## The 8 Dimensions

Computed from planetary positions at birth. Each maps to a psychological force in the Jungian tradition:

| Symbol | Dimension | Psychological Domain | Planet |
|--------|-----------|----------------------|--------|
| ☀ | **Identity** | The shape of the self; what seeks expression | Sun |
| ♄ | **Structure** | Necessity; what you must build and maintain | Saturn |
| ☿ | **Mind** | The mental function; directed attention | Mercury |
| ♀ | **Heart** | Eros; what you value and how you relate to beauty | Venus |
| ♃ | **Growth** | Expansion; the narrative of becoming | Jupiter |
| ♂ | **Drive** | Libido; the energy that wants to act | Mars |
| ☽ | **Connection** | The relational field; bonds and empathy | Moon |
| ♅ | **Awareness** | The transcendent function; what exceeds the ego | Uranus/Neptune |

## The Core Loop

```
Birth data → 8D natal vector → archetype
                                    ↓
                       Daily reflection (5 questions)
                                    ↓
                   Field coherence score (κ) + delta
                                    ↓
                   Sol's reading — opens with today's check-in
                                    ↓
                        "A question to carry today"
                                    ↓
                             Come back tomorrow
```

The critical word is **opens with**. Sol does not read your natal chart and then consult your check-in. Sol reads what you just reported — "Contracted," "Scatters immediately," "Numb or disconnected" — and names the withdrawn dimension as a psychological reality. The natal pattern and current transit provide depth, not the starting point.

---

## Sol's Voice

Sol works in the tradition of Jungian depth psychology — Liz Greene in sensibility, not style. Planets as autonomous psychological forces. Transits as moments when something unconscious seeks to become conscious.

**Voice rules:**
- Never prescribe. Never advise. Observe and illuminate.
- No sign names, no degree positions.
- No affirmations, no self-help language, no therapeutic reassurance.
- Allowed: shadow, projection, individuation, fate, necessity — used precisely.

## What We Are Not Building

- A prediction engine. We describe what is moving, not what will happen.
- A therapy app. Sol illuminates; it does not heal.
- A gamification system. The streak exists to establish the practice, not to be the practice.
- A social network. The soul field share feature is an artifact, not a feed.
